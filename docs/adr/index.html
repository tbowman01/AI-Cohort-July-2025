üìñ About ADRs

Architecture Decision Records document important architectural decisions made during project development. Each ADR includes:

  • Context: The situation that requires a decision
  • Decision: The architectural decision chosen
  • Consequences: The positive and negative impacts of the decision
  • Alternatives: Other options considered and why they were rejected
